Characters

There are a total of 35 playable characters in Genshin Impact. Mihoyo has also confirmed the upcoming release of 10 new characters.
How to Obtain
Characters can be obtained from wishing with Intertwined or Aquaint Fates which are purchased with primogems that are obtained through various means.
They can also be bought in Paimon's Shop or can be earned in a few events and the Spiral Abyss.
When wishing, the chances of obtaining a certain character or rarity changes based on the amount of wishes and the specific banner.
In the standard banner, every 10 wishes a 4 star item or character is pulled, every 90 wishes, a 5 star character or item is pulled.
Constellations
All characters have 6 constellations, these constellations give various buffs and changes.
Constellations are obtained when players pull duplicate characters from wishes or other means.
The Travelers constellations are obtained through other means because he/she cannot be obtained multiple times.
